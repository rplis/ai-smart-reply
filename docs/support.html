Support — Smart Reply

Smart Reply is an AI-powered Outlook add-in by R-Technologies that suggests replies to the email you are reading. Use this page to get help and contact us.

How to use Smart Reply

  1. Open a message in Outlook (read view).
  2. Open the Smart Reply task pane from the ribbon.
  3. Review the suggested replies or generate alternatives as shown in the add-in.
  4. Edit the text before sending, if your workflow requires it.

FAQ

The add-in does not load or the task pane is blank.
Check your internet connection. Ask your IT admin whether Outlook add-ins are allowed and whether your network allows access to the Smart Reply endpoints. Try restarting Outlook.

Suggested replies do not appear or requests fail.
Ensure you are signed in to Microsoft 365 as expected. If your organization uses licensing or tokens for Smart Reply, confirm your subscription is active. Retry after a few minutes in case of temporary service maintenance.

I see an error about authentication or license.
Your account may not be authorized for Smart Reply, or the token may have expired. Contact your administrator or reach out to us using the contacts below.
