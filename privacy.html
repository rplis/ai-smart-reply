Privacy Policy — AI Smart Reply

Last updated: April 1, 2026 · Provider: R-Technologies

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Smart Reply (the Outlook add-in and its backend service, together “AI Smart Reply” or “the service”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the service. It applies to AI Smart Reply as a product, not to unrelated websites.

1. Who we are

AI Smart Reply is provided by R-Technologies. For privacy-related questions, contact: rafal@aiwtwojejfirmie.com.pl.

2. What AI Smart Reply does

AI Smart Reply is an AI-powered assistant for Microsoft Outlook that helps you draft replies to email messages. When you use AI Smart Reply, the service processes the content needed to generate suggested replies (for example, the body and subject of the message you are viewing, and related context you choose to send).

3. Information we process

Depending on how you use AI Smart Reply, we may process:

  • Email content you submit to the service — text from the open message (and optional fields such as subject or sender) that you send to our API so we can classify the message and generate reply suggestions.
  • Authentication and access — security tokens or credentials your client sends so we can verify access to the service (for example, an API token).
  • Account and licensing — your Microsoft 365–related email address or identifier when required to validate subscription or license status for AI Smart Reply.
  • Technical data — standard server logs (for example, timestamps, IP addresses, error codes) needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service.

AI Smart Reply runs inside Outlook and uses permissions you grant in Microsoft 365. We do not use this Privacy Policy to describe Microsoft’s own data practices; see Microsoft’s privacy statements for how Outlook and Microsoft 365 handle your data.

4. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Provide reply generation and related features of AI Smart Reply;
  • Authenticate requests and enforce access and licensing rules;
  • Improve reliability, security, and performance of the service;
  • Comply with legal obligations where applicable.

Email content sent to our backend may be processed by Azure OpenAI (or comparable AI services we configure) to produce suggestions. Processing is done to fulfill your request; we do not use your messages for unrelated advertising profiling through AI Smart Reply.

5. Automated processing and sensitive data

The service may apply automated steps (including redaction of certain patterns) before sending content to AI models. You should avoid pasting unnecessary sensitive personal data into replies. If your organization has rules for handling guest or employee data, follow those policies when using AI Smart Reply.

6. Retention

AI Smart Reply is designed so that email content you send for generation is processed to return a result and is not kept for long-term storage as part of the core reply workflow. Server logs may be retained for a limited period for security and operations. Retention details may vary by deployment; contact us if you need specifics for your tenant.

7. Sharing and subprocessors

We use service providers that help run AI Smart Reply, including Microsoft Azure (hosting, identity-related services as applicable) and Azure OpenAI for AI inference. These providers process data on our behalf under appropriate agreements and safeguards.

8. International transfers

Data may be processed in the European Economic Area and/or other regions where Microsoft Azure and related services operate, in line with applicable transfer mechanisms (such as Standard Contractual Clauses where required).

9. Your rights (EEA/UK)

If applicable law grants you rights (for example access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, or data portability), you may exercise them by contacting us at the email above. You may also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

10. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect information processed by AI Smart Reply, including transport encryption where supported by the client and API.

11. Children

AI Smart Reply is not directed at children. Do not use the service for accounts that are not permitted by your organization or applicable law.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of AI Smart Reply after changes means you accept the updated policy.
